Front-end Developer & Designer

A front-end developer and designer is a professional who specializes in creating the user interface and visual presentation of a website or application. They are responsible for implementing designs, coding the front-end structure using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and ensuring a seamless user experience. They collaborate with designers to translate visual concepts into functional interfaces and optimize the performance and responsiveness of the website or application. In short, they combine design and coding skills to create visually appealing and user-friendly digital experiences.
